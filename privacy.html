Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 24, 2026

Summary: NutriAI does not collect, store, or share any personal data on external servers. All your information stays on your device. We use Google AdMob for advertising, which may collect limited data as described below.

1. Introduction

Welcome to NutriAI ("we", "our", "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application NutriAI (the "App"), available on Android, iOS, and Web platforms.

We are committed to protecting your privacy. By using the App, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

To generate personalized meal plans, you may optionally provide the following data within the App:

  • Physical data: sex, age, weight, and height (for automatic macro calculations)
  • Activity level and fitness goals (lose, maintain, or gain weight)
  • Food preferences: favorite foods, disliked foods, allergies, and intolerances
  • Meal plan configuration: number of days, meals per day, and variety preferences

Important: All of this data is stored exclusively on your device using local storage (SharedPreferences). We do not transmit, collect, or have access to any of this information. No accounts or registration are required.

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

The App itself does not collect any analytics, usage data, crash reports, or device identifiers. However, third-party services integrated into the App may collect limited data as described in Section 4.

3. How We Use Your Information

The personal data you enter into the App (body metrics, food preferences, etc.) is used solely for the following purposes:

  • Calculating your Basal Metabolic Rate (BMR) and macronutrient targets
  • Generating personalized meal plans tailored to your preferences
  • Creating shopping lists based on your generated meal plans
  • Saving your preferences and meal plans locally so they persist between sessions

This data never leaves your device and is not shared with us or any third party.

4. Third-Party Services

4.1 Google AdMob

We use Google AdMob to display advertisements in the App. Google AdMob may collect and process certain information, including:

  • Device identifiers (advertising ID)
  • IP address
  • General device information (model, OS version)
  • Ad interaction data (impressions, clicks)

This data is collected and processed by Google in accordance with Google's Privacy Policy. We do not have access to the personal data collected by Google AdMob.

Users in the European Economic Area (EEA) and the UK will be shown a consent dialog for personalized ads in compliance with GDPR regulations. You may opt out of personalized advertising through your device's settings.

5. Data Storage and Security

All user-provided data is stored locally on your device using the operating system's standard local storage mechanisms. We do not operate any servers, databases, or cloud storage that hold user data.

  • Storage location: Your device only (SharedPreferences / local storage)
  • Data transmission: None — no data is sent to external servers by us
  • Data retention: Data persists until you clear the App's data, uninstall the App, or clear your browser storage

6. Children's Privacy

NutriAI is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The App does not require account creation or any form of registration.

Since all data is stored locally on the device and is never transmitted to us, we have no means of identifying or collecting data from any specific user, including minors.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided information through the App that concerns you, please contact us, and we will assist you.

7. Your Rights

Since we do not collect or store your data on any server, the following applies:

  • Access and Deletion: All your data is on your device. You can delete it at any time by clearing the App's data in your device settings or by uninstalling the App.
  • Portability: Your meal plans and preferences are stored locally and are not transferable between devices.
  • Ad Preferences: You can opt out of personalized advertising through your device's advertising settings (Settings → Privacy → Advertising on iOS, or Settings → Google → Ads on Android).

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. We encourage you to review this page periodically.

If we make significant changes that affect how your data is handled, we will notify users through an update to the App or by posting a prominent notice on this page.
