The Science

Built on real nutrition science

Every calculation in NutriAI follows established nutritional research and evidence-based formulas used by dietitians worldwide.

🧮 Macro Calculator Mifflin-St Jeor

We use the Mifflin-St Jeor equation — the gold standard for estimating Basal Metabolic Rate (BMR) — combined with activity multipliers and goal-based adjustments.

  • ✓ Sex, age, weight & height considered
  • ✓ 5 activity levels (sedentary → very active)
  • ✓ Goal-adjusted: lose (-20%), maintain, gain (+15%)
  • ✓ Protein: 1.8–2.2g per kg of body weight
  • ✓ Fat: 25–28% of total calories
  • ✓ Remaining calories allocated to carbs

🍽️ Smart Meal Engine Constraint-Based

Our meal planner uses a multi-constraint algorithm that respects your preferences while hitting your exact nutritional targets.

  • ✓ Allergen & intolerance filtering
  • ✓ Breakfast-specific food selection
  • ✓ Lighter dinners with more vegetables
  • ✓ Portion scaling to match calorie targets
  • ✓ Variety enforcement across days
  • ✓ Auto-correction if daily deviation > 5%

FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know about NutriAI.

Is NutriAI free to use? +
Yes! NutriAI is completely free. You can generate unlimited meal plans, manage your preferences, and create shopping lists at no cost. The app is supported by non-intrusive banner ads.
How does the macro calculation work? +
NutriAI uses the Mifflin-St Jeor equation to calculate your Basal Metabolic Rate (BMR), then adjusts for your activity level to find your Total Daily Energy Expenditure (TDEE). Based on your goal (lose, maintain, or gain weight), it distributes calories across protein, carbs, and fat according to evidence-based guidelines.
Can I use it if I have food allergies? +
Absolutely. NutriAI supports 10+ allergen types including gluten, lactose, nuts, shellfish, eggs, soy, fish, and more. You can even set exceptions within allergen groups — for example, mark a specific fish as safe even if you're generally avoiding seafood.
What platforms does NutriAI work on? +
NutriAI works on iOS, Android, Web, macOS, Linux, and Windows. It's built with Flutter for a consistent experience across all platforms. You're using the web version right now!
Are the meal plans saved? +
Yes. Your generated meal plans are saved locally on your device, so you can close the app and come back anytime without losing your current plan. You can also regenerate a new plan whenever you want.
